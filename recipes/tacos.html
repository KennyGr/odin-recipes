Tacos

Description

Tacos are probably the most delicious dish that exists. It's pretty unfortunate that you can't really get them anywhere in Japan.

Ingredients

  • Taco Shells
  • Taco Sauce
  • Ground Beef or Chicken
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook your meat of choice until edible.
  2. Fill a taco shell with some of the cooked meat.
  3. Place preferred toppings on top of the meat (if adding cheese, it is recommended to add it directly on top of the meat, as this will melt the cheese. Nothing is worse than unmelted cheese on top of lettuce.)
